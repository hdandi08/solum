## Concept

Social proof with a self-aware wink. The viewer IS the confident man — or is becoming him. He knows she noticed. He knows exactly why. The humor is in the smug understatement: his secret is a back scrub cloth and a clay mask.

**Pillar:** THE RESULT (aspirational, grounded)
**Tone:** Dry confidence. Playful without being cringe. The joke is implied, never stated.
**Brand level:** Full SOLUM ritual — no single product focus.

## Layout Rules

- **Safe zone:** All text positioned between 15% and 85% of viewport height. No text near top (Instagram UI) or bottom (caption/interaction bar).
- All scenes: `<div class="brand-rule">` (3px steel blue left edge) + `<div class="scene-wordmark">SOLUM</div>` (top-left ambient, 16px, opacity 0.6) — positioned within safe zone.
- Text centred horizontally and vertically on all scenes.

---

## Animation

- S1, S2: Standard `.txt` fade + translateY entry.
- S3: Three `.txt` blocks with staggered `data-delay` (e.g. 0ms, 400ms, 800ms). Each line enters separately.
- S4: Single `.txt.pos-centre` wrapper. Line 2 uses child class with `transition-delay: 600ms`, triggered by `#s4 .txt.visible .s4-line2`.
- S5: Standard CTA entry.
