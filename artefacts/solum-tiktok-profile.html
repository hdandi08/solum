9:16 vertical · 1080×1920 · 15–60 seconds

Always on. Auto-captions enabled.
85% of TikTok is watched without sound first. Captions are not optional — they are the hook delivery mechanism. TikTok's auto-caption is good enough. Turn it on every video.
