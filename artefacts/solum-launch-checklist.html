  • 4 Scarcity is real — say it. 150 kits is a real constraint. "150 kits · Spaces filling fast" is not a dark pattern — it's honest. Use it in ads, on the site, in emails.
  • 5 Post organic every day. Organic views seed your retargeting pool at zero cost. Each reel you post = more people who've heard of SOLUM before they see a paid ad. Compound effect builds within days.
