## 3. THE MESSAGING FRAMEWORK

Every piece of content runs this ladder in order. Never skip a layer, never reorder.

| Layer | Name | What it does | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **The Wrong** | Names the problem. Makes him feel it. Urgency. | *"Your back has never been clean."* |
| 2 | **The Why** | Removes blame. Nobody built the system. | *"Not your fault. Nobody taught you this."* |
| 3 | **The Fix** | SOLUM. Concrete and specific. | *"10 minutes. 8 products. Two rituals."* |
| 4 | **The Proof** | Physical outcomes. Tangible, not aspirational. | *"Week 1: dead skin rolls off. Week 2: odour reduces."* |
| 5 | **The Identity** | Who they become. The keep layer. | *"Your body. Done right."* |

**By format:**
- A single Instagram post hits layers 1–2
- An ad hits layers 1–3
- The full website hits all 5

**The first emotional reaction must be urgency.** Not aspiration. Not curiosity. Urgency.

### Tone rules
- Empathetic on cause: "Nobody built men this system" — not "men don't care"
- Confrontational on result: "You've been getting this wrong" — not "you might want to consider"
- Specific, never vague: "Dead skin feeds odour bacteria" — not "feel fresher"
- Numbers anchor truth: "70% more moisture in 3 minutes" — not "deeply hydrating"
- Short sentences. Declarative. No hedging.

## 4. INSTAGRAM STRATEGY

**Platform:** Instagram only. @bysolum (or handle TBC).

**Approach:** Education-first. Expose the gap in men's body care knowledge. Never lead with products — lead with the problem.

**Macro hook for all content:**
> Most men shower every day. Most men still have rough skin. These aren't unrelated.

### 5 Content Pillars

| Pillar | Theme | Tone |
|---|---|---|
| THE GAP | Facts men don't know about their skin/body | Confrontational, direct |
| THE RITUAL | How to use products correctly | Instructional, confident |
| THE SCIENCE | Why ingredients/techniques work | Educational, credible |
| THE ORIGIN | Where products come from (Morocco, Korea, Turkey) | Storytelling, authentic |
| THE RESULT | Before/after framing, transformation | Aspirational, grounded |

### Posting cadence
- Target: 3 posts per week (scale to 5)
- Monday: Static image — THE GAP
- Wednesday: Reel 15–30s — THE RITUAL or THE SCIENCE
- Friday: Carousel 3–5 slides — THE RITUAL step-by-step

### Caption rules
- Always open with Layer 1 (The Wrong). Never start with "SOLUM". Never start with a product name.
- Short sentences. Declarative. No fluff.
- Always end with a question or CTA that earns a comment or save
- Max 5 hashtags per post/reel. Mix: #mensgrooming #skincareformen #bodycare #menswellness #groomingtips
- Never "buy now" in the awareness phase — CTA is always "link in bio" pointing to bysolum.com

### Funnel structure
- Posts 1–6: Pure awareness. No products. Earn trust.
- Posts 7–12: The system exists. Tease the ritual.
- Posts 13+: Specific products. The SOLUM method.

## 5. REEL FORMAT SPECIFICATION

All reels follow the same 5-scene structure. 20–22 seconds total. No talking head — text on black only.

### Scene structure
| Scene | Duration | Purpose | Visual treatment |
|---|---|---|---|
| S1 — Hook | 2.5s | The Wrong. One confrontational statement. | Big Bebas Neue text, bone on near-black |
| S2 — Setup | 3s | The Why or elaboration. Still big text. | Clean, minimal |
| S3 — Hero | 7s | The Proof or Reveal. Most time, most drama. | Staggered list, scroll reveal, or contrast breakdown |
| S4 — Statement | 4s | The damage or the fix. Two lines, staggered. | First line drops, second follows with delay |
| S5 — CTA | 4s | SOLUM wordmark + product name + bysolum.com | Radial glow background, full brand lockup |

### Visual identity (locked — never change)
**Fonts:**
- Headlines/hooks: Bebas Neue, letterspacing 0.15em, always uppercase
- Subheadings/labels: Barlow Condensed 700
- Body/effects: Barlow Condensed 600
- Small captions: Barlow Condensed 500

**Colours:**
| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| SOLUM Black | #08090B | Primary background |
| Charcoal | #181C24 | UI surface |
| Deep Blue | #1A4A78 | Dark accent |
| Steel Blue | #2E6DA4 | Primary accent / brand rule / progress bar |
| Sky Blue | #4A8FC7 | Highlight / effects text |
| Bone | #F0ECE2 | Primary type colour |

**Layout:**
- 405×720px canvas (9:16 for Instagram Reels)
- 3px steel blue vertical rule on the left edge — on every scene
- SOLUM wordmark ambient watermark top-left, 16px Bebas, opacity 0.6 — on every scene
- Text safe zone: content must stay within 40px of either side edge (Instagram crops in Reels fullscreen)

### Text size minimums (non-negotiable — smaller = illegible on phone)
| Element | Minimum size |

| Hook / headline | 60px Bebas Neue (72–90px preferred) |

## 6. WHAT HAS BEEN TESTED (AD PERFORMANCE DATA)

All reels run as paid Instagram ads. Primary KPI is CPV — cost per profile visit. Cost per view is nearly identical across all reels (~£0.01). The gap between reels is driven entirely by view → profile visit conversion rate.

| # | Hook | Spend | Views | Profile Visits | CPV | View→Visit Rate | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Reel 03 | "You shower everyday, your body still smells" | £15 | 1,439 | 74 | **£0.20** | **5.1%** | **Champion** |
| Reel 05 | "Your scalp is skin. You've never once cleaned it" | £8 | 790 | 32 | £0.25 | 4.1% | Strong |
| Reel 04 | "Your body wash is stripping your skin" | £14 | 1,333 | 45 | £0.31 | 3.4% | Good |
| Reel 06 (est) | "You've been showering wrong" | £8 | 575 | 21 | £0.38 | 3.7% | Weak |
| Reel 07 | "She noticed" | unknown | unknown | unknown | dead | ~2s avg view | Dead |
| Reel 11 | "Your morning routine is undoing itself" | £4.87 | 214 | 4 | **£1.22** | **1.9%** | **Dead — worst ever** |

### What the data proves

**Formula that works:**
> You do [daily habit] but [invisible bad consequence] is happening to you. You don't know.

**Hook rankings by type:**
1. Invisible personal consequence (smell, skin condition you can't see) → £0.20 CPV
2. Specific body part they've never thought about (scalp) → £0.25 CPV
3. Process/mechanism they're doing wrong (stripping, ingredients) → £0.31 CPV
4. Vague accusation ("showering wrong") → £0.38 CPV
5. Abstract/metaphor hooks ("undoing itself") → £1.22 CPV — fails badly
6. Social proof hooks ("she noticed") → dead

**What fails and why:** Abstract or conceptual hooks lose viewers before 3 seconds. "Undoing itself" — only 35% made it to 3 seconds. The hook must name the viewer and name their personal consequence immediately. No metaphors, no implied outcomes, no aspiration gap.

**Key insight:** META algorithm reaches similar audiences at similar cost regardless of reel quality. £0.01 per view is consistent across all reels. The entire performance gap is hook quality → view-to-visit conversion.

## 8. AD STRATEGY

**Primary KPI:** CPV — cost per profile visit. Target: beat £0.20.
**Budget model:** £5/reel/day for 3 days per test = ~£30 per reel test
**Kill rule:** Stop any reel at £0.50+ CPV by day 2 — it's dead, don't waste budget
**A/B test setup:** Champion reel vs one challenger, equal budget, same audience, same 3-day duration

**Target audience (Meta Ads):** UK men, ages 22–40, interests: grooming, skincare, fitness, wellness

**Champion/Challenger model:**
- The best-performing reel (currently reel-03 at £0.20 CPV) runs continuously
- Each week, one new challenger is tested against it
- If the challenger beats the champion, it becomes the new champion
- Always be testing — never just running one reel

**Current champion:** Reel-03 — "You shower every day. You still smell." — £0.20 CPV
