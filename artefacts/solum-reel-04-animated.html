How to record
1. Press CMD + SHIFT + 5 → "Record Selected Portion" → draw selection around the reel frame
2. Click "Record Mode" — countdown appears, stage fills screen
3. Hit Record in QuickTime during the countdown
4. Reel plays automatically after countdown ends
5. Stop QuickTime after Scene 5 fades out
