Daily Ritual
● Every Day · 10 Minutes
THE DAILY ROUTINE.
Every shower — no exceptions
1
04 · Scalp Massager
Scalp massage — during shampoo
Place flat on scalp, work in small firm circles from hairline to back. 2–3 minutes. Most men have never done this once. You'll feel your scalp warm — that's circulation.
2
01 · Body Wash
Full body cleanse — head to toe
Apply to wet skin, head to toe including all sensitive areas. No sulphates — lifts dirt without stripping your skin's natural oils. Clean skin feels clean. Not tight. Not dry.
3
02 · Nylon Scrub Cloth
Exfoliate — full body including back
Wet cloth, add body wash. Long circular strokes on arms, chest, stomach, legs. Drape over shoulder for the back. Nylon is safe for daily use — replace every 90 days.
4
09 · Intimate Cleansing Cloth
Separate cloth. Separate step.
With body wash. Dedicated cloth for sensitive areas. The area most men ignore is the area that needs the most attention. Replace monthly.
5
07 · Body Lotion
The 3-minute window — don't skip
Apply within 3 minutes of towelling. Skin absorbs significantly more moisture while still warm. Two pumps. Press in, don't rub. Get dressed straight after.
Weekly Hammam Ritual
● Once A Week · 25 Minutes
THE HAMMAM SEQUENCE.
Replace your daily routine — once a week. Four steps. All Morocco.
1
05 · Rhassoul Clay + 06 · Argan Oil + 04 · Scalp Massager
Clay on body. Oil on scalp. Massager working simultaneously.
Apply clay to damp skin neck to feet. Then 4–5 drops of argan oil on your scalp — work it in with the scalp massager. Firm slow circles, every section. The clay is pulling impurities from your skin. The oil is feeding your scalp. Both happening at once. Leave 8–10 minutes.
2
03 · Viscose Scrub Cloth
Deep exfoliation — scrub the clay off
One handle each hand. Drape over shoulder. Firm slow strokes — upper, mid, lower back. Then front. The viscose is coarser than your daily nylon — built for this moment. The clay has already loosened everything. You will see dead skin lift. That is the point.
3
06 · Argan Oil
Your skin is open right now. Seal it.
Rinse. Towel lightly. Apply 10–15 drops to damp skin immediately. Press in — don't rub. The clay opened everything. The viscose cleared it. The oil seals it. This is the step most men never take. It's why your skin will feel different.
4
07 · Body Lotion
Finish — the 3-minute window
Skin is freshly exfoliated — absorption is at its peak. Two pumps. Press in. Don't waste the window.
