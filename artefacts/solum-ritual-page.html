How to do it

Exfoliating Mitt — Korean Technique
3
Minutes
02
Video coming soon
~60 sec
How to do it
1
Slip the mitt over your hand and wet it fully — a saturated mitt glides, a dry one scratches.
2
Add 2 pumps of body wash directly onto the mitt surface.
3
Arms: long upward strokes from wrist to shoulder. Rotate the arm to cover all sides.
4
Chest and stomach: wide circular strokes with medium pressure.
5
Legs: long upward strokes from ankle to thigh, covering the full leg on each pass.
6
Pressure: firm but no discomfort — feel resistance, not pain. Never use on the face.
7
After use: rinse the mitt thoroughly and hang flat to air dry.
Korean bathhouse technique
The Italy Towel is the most effective exfoliation tool in the world. Long strokes — not scrubbing — lift dead skin without damaging the live layer beneath. You may see buildup on first use. This is normal and will reduce week by week.

Back Scrub Cloth
2
Minutes
03
Video coming soon
~45 sec
How to do it
1
Hold one handle in each hand. Wet the cloth fully — it needs to be saturated.
2
Add one pump of body wash to the centre of the cloth.
3
Throw the cloth over one shoulder so it hangs flat against your back.
4
Alternate pulling each handle in a sawing motion — like drying your back with a towel, but deliberate and firm.
5
Work in three zones: upper back (20 sec), mid back (20 sec), lower back (20 sec). Repeat.
6
Rinse cloth thoroughly. Hang to dry with both handles free and cloth fully extended.
The only tool that reaches
Your back is the largest surface on your body and the most neglected. Hands don't reach. This cloth does. Every pass covers skin that's been ignored for years.

Body Lotion — The 3-Minute Window
2
Minutes
07
Video coming soon
~45 sec
How to do it
1
Step out of the shower. Towel off lightly — remove dripping water but your skin should remain warm and slightly damp.
2
You have 3 minutes. Warm, slightly damp skin absorbs 70% more moisture than dry skin. Do not wait.
3
Pump twice into your palm. Start at the calves and work upward.
4
Press, don't rub. Both palms flat into the skin, hold 2 seconds, move up and repeat.
5
Work upward: thighs → stomach → chest → arms. Add a second pump for the top half.
6
No additional moisturiser needed. The ritual is complete.
The 3-minute window
This is not a slogan. The hydration gradient between warm post-shower skin and the lotion is what drives absorption. Let that window close and you've left the most important step half-done.

Product 05 · Rhassoul Clay · Weekly Deep
Rhassoul Clay Body Mask
10
Minutes
05
Video coming soon
~90 sec
Mixing the clay — do this before you shower
1
Scoop 2–3 tablespoons of clay into a bowl before getting in the shower.
2
Add warm water slowly — a small amount at a time. Stir between each addition.
3
Target consistency: thick yoghurt — spreadable but holds its shape. If it runs off a spoon it's too thin; add more clay.
Applying the clay
4
Enter the shower and warm your body — but do not rinse yet. Warm, barely damp skin, not wet skin.
5
Apply clay with your hands from neck to feet — chest, stomach, arms, legs. Use the exfoliating mitt to reach your back.
6
Leave for 8–10 minutes. You will feel it tighten as it works — that means the clay is drawing impurities out.
7
Do not let it dry out fully — if it cracks and goes white, mist with warm water from the shower head.
8
Rinse thoroughly with warm water. Move immediately to the Kese Mitt step — your skin is now fully prepared.
Atlas Mountain clay
Rhassoul clay has one of the highest ion exchange capacities of any clay mineral — it binds to excess oil, impurities and dead cells and pulls them out as it dries. 8 minutes is the minimum for this process to complete fully.

How to do it
1

Hand-woven Turkish raw silk creates more friction than viscose. The clay has already lifted the dead layer — the Kese removes it completely. The combination of clay + Kese is the foundation of the Turkish hammam tradition, unchanged for centuries.

How to do it
1
Step out of the shower. Towel off lightly — stay damp. Do not dry completely.

6
No body lotion today. The argan oil is your complete moisture barrier. The weekly ritual is complete.
