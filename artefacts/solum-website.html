Questions

Answered
Plainly.

Am I paying £78 every month?+
No. £78 is a one-time payment for your first box, which includes physical tools that last 6–12 months. After that, you pay £44/month for consumables only — body wash, lotion, and clay. The tools are already in your home. You never pay £78 again unless you want another full kit.
Is this for my face or my body?+
Your body. Entirely. SOLUM is the first serious body care system for men — it does not replace your face routine, shampoo, or deodorant. It addresses skin from your neck down: exfoliation, back care, scalp health, and daily moisturisation of the body. The 90% of your skin that most products ignore.
I've never used anything on my body. Is this too advanced?+
This is built specifically for that person. The Daily Starter is 3 products, numbered in order. The ritual card tells you exactly when to use each one and for how long. If you can remember to shower, you can follow this. Start with Daily Starter — upgrade to Full Ritual when it becomes automatic.
Why does it matter that I use the lotion within 3 minutes?+
Immediately after showering, your skin is warm and the outer layer is still hydrated. Moisture absorption is significantly higher during this window. Wait 15 minutes and you've largely missed it — the lotion sits on top rather than absorbing. The 3-minute rule is dermatology, not marketing.
Can I cancel or pause my subscription?+
Yes. Any time. One click. No penalty, no phone calls, no retention flows designed to confuse you. Skip a month if you're travelling. Pause indefinitely. We'd rather you come back when you're ready than resent us for charging you when you don't need it.
Does it work as a gift?+
The World Kit is designed for gifting. Rigid matte black box, steel blue foil strip, ribbon pull, ritual card face-up. It looks like something that cost significantly more than it did. You can choose whether to include a subscription with it or let the recipient decide after they've tried it.
