Before You Read These Numbers
A Note On Projections
These are cohort-modelled projections — not guesses, not best-case fantasies. Every number flows from locked unit economics: £62.78 gross profit per Full kit, £30.35/month sub gross profit, £28 CAC, 7% monthly churn. Two kits: Basic (£72) and Full (£89). Sub COGS includes quarterly cloth replacements (nylon + viscose every 90 days). The model simulates each month independently, churning existing subscribers before adding new ones.

What these projections cannot account for: supplier delays, fulfilment failures, ad platform changes, product quality issues, or the general chaos of building a business. The conservative scenario is the one to plan from. The base case is the one to aim for. The optimistic case is what happens if your Instagram content works and churn drops below 6%.

The single most important number in this entire model is not revenue — it is monthly churn rate. Dropping from 7% to 5.5% churn (base → optimistic) nearly triples 5-year profit. No amount of marketing spend comes close to that leverage.

The Four Levers That Drive Everything
Lever 01 — The Most Important
Churn Rate
The difference between conservative and optimistic is almost entirely churn — 7.5% vs 5.5%. That 2pp difference produces a £18.5M swing in 5-year profit. No marketing budget can replicate this leverage. Every product decision, every piece of packaging, every ritual card exists to serve one goal: keeping subscribers past month 6.
Lever 02 — Best Risk-Adjusted Move
Organic Mix
Raising organic acquisition from 20% to 35% (conservative → optimistic) means effectively doubling your paid budget for free. This is what Instagram content does. Every viral hygiene education post reduces your effective CAC. At scale, organic is the difference between a good business and an exceptional one.
Lever 03 — Paid Growth Engine
Ad Spend
Because CAC payback is Month 1, every pound of ad spend is recovered immediately. This means you can reinvest gross profit directly back into acquisition without needing external capital. The model is self-funding from Year 1. The question is simply how fast you want to grow.
Lever 04 — The Mix Upgrade
Kit Mix
Moving customers from Basic (£72) to Full (£89) increases first-order GP by £11.50 and monthly sub GP from £23.85 to £30.35. Full subscribers generate £86 more lifetime value. The pricing gap is intentional — the argan oil is the single differentiator and it delivers.
