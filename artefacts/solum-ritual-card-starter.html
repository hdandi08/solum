THE DAILY ROUTINE.
Every morning — after your shower
1
04 · Scalp Massager
Scalp massage — during shampoo
Place flat on scalp, work in small firm circles from hairline to back. 2–3 minutes. Most men have never done this once. You'll feel your scalp warm — that's circulation.
2
01 · Body Wash
Cleanse without stripping
Apply to wet skin, chest down to feet. No sulphates — lifts dirt without removing your skin's natural oils. Clean skin feels clean. Not tight. Not dry.
3
02 · Nylon Scrub Cloth
Exfoliate — full body including back
Wet the cloth, add a little body wash. Long circular strokes on arms, chest, stomach, legs. One handle each hand — drape over your shoulder for the back. Medium pressure. Safe for daily use.
4
09 · Intimate Cleansing Cloth
Separate cloth. Separate step.
With body wash. Dedicated cloth for sensitive areas. The area most men ignore is the area that needs the most attention. Replace monthly.
5
07 · Body Lotion
The 3-minute window — don't skip this
Apply within 3 minutes of towelling. Skin absorbs significantly more moisture while still warm. Two pumps. Press in, don't rub. Get dressed straight after.
