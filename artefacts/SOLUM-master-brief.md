## 1. BRAND IDENTITY

**Name:** SOLUM  
**Tagline:** Your body. Done right.  
**Category:** Men's body care — not face, not hair. Head to toe.  
**Core truth:** Most men shower every day and still have rough skin, a neglected back, and a scalp they've never once properly cleaned. Not laziness — nobody ever built them a system worth following. SOLUM fixes that.

### Brand Rules — NEVER BREAK
- Never lowercase the wordmark
- Never use the word "soap" — not in copy, not on labels, not anywhere
- Never orange, amber, yellow, or green
- Never Arabic script
- Always number products 01–08
- Always include country of origin on every label
- No figure or person on any box
- Products 02 + 03: Do NOT claim "Made in Korea" — they are manufactured in China. Label copy references Korean bathhouse tradition honestly.

## 3. TWO-TIER RITUAL SYSTEM

### Daily Ritual — 10 Minutes (Every Morning)
1. **Product 04** — Scalp massage during shampoo. Small firm circles, hairline to back. 2–3 min.
2. **Product 01** — Body wash, chest down. No sulphates.
3. **Product 02** — Exfoliating mitt, front of body. Long circular strokes, medium pressure.
4. **Product 03** — Back cloth, both handles. Saw back and forth, upper/mid/lower. 60 seconds.
5. **Product 07** — Body lotion within 3 minutes of towelling. Two pumps, press in.

### Weekly Deep Ritual — 22 Minutes (Replaces Daily)
1. **Product 04** — Deep scalp massage, 5 minutes.
2. **Product 05** — Rhassoul clay full body, leave 8–10 minutes on damp skin.
3. **Product 08 + 03** — Kese mitt (front) + back cloth simultaneously. Firm slow strokes. Dead skin will roll off.
4. **Product 06** — Argan oil on damp skin immediately after. 10–15 drops, press in. No lotion needed.
