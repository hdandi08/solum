## Core Angle: The System Gap

**The macro hook:** Most men shower every day. Most men still have rough skin. These aren't unrelated.

The gap is that nobody ever built men a body system worth following. SOLUM doesn't lecture men about hygiene — it gives them the first system worth following. That's the frame for every piece of content.

**Tone:** The gym mate who actually knows what he's talking about. Direct. Zero fluff. Slightly confrontational but never preachy.

**Never use gender comparisons** ("women have a routine, men don't") — not all women have elaborate routines, the framing is borrowed and overused, and it shames by comparison. Keep the focus on men, for men.

**Never say:** skincare, pamper, self-care, cleanse (in a soft way), nourish
**Always say:** system, ritual, method, works, does the job, built for

## 5 Content Pillars

| Pillar | Theme | Tone | Hook Style |
|---|---|---|---|
| **THE SYSTEM GAP** | Women have rituals. Men have nothing. Here's why. | Confrontational, direct | Contrast / stat that lands |
| **THE METHOD** | This is how it's done. Specific, no fluff. | Instructional, confident | "Most men do X. This is wrong." |
| **THE PROOF** | Numbers and facts that make you take this seriously | Credible, blunt | Stat first, explanation second |
| **THE CRAFT** | Where SOLUM sources from and why it matters | Storytelling, authentic | Origin / heritage angle |
| **WHAT CHANGES** | Not before/after — "you'll notice" framing | Grounded, no hype | Specifics, not superlatives |

## Pre-Launch Content Rules (No Products Yet)

**Do:**
- Pure education posts — stock imagery, text-on-screen, brand voice only
- Founder POV — "building SOLUM from scratch, here's what I'm learning"
- Origin/craft content — Moroccan hammam, Korean bathhouse, Turkish hamam (all on stock sites)
- Tease the gap — position what doesn't exist yet, build intrigue

**Don't:**
- Show the actual product until samples arrive
- How-to content that requires product footage
- Before/after claims without evidence

## Format Rules

### Static Posts
- Background: SOLUM Black (#08090B)
- Headline: Bebas Neue, letterspacing 0.15em, all caps, Bone (#F0ECE2)
- Body (if any): Barlow Condensed 300, Bone
- Accent: Steel Blue (#2E6DA4) for one word or line max
- One idea per post. No clutter.

### Reels (15–30s)
- Structure: **Hook (2s) → Problem (5s) → Reframe/SOLUM (10–15s)**
- B-roll stock footage + text-on-screen + trending audio
- No talking head pre-launch
- End card: SOLUM wordmark + bysolum.com

### Carousels (3–5 slides)
- Slide 1 = scroll-stopper hook
- Slides 2–4 = the method / proof / steps
- Slide 5 = SOLUM + CTA ("The system is at bysolum.com")
- Carousels are saved more than any other format — use for depth

---

## Caption Voice Rules

- Short sentences. No fluff. Declarative.
- Never "pamper yourself" — always "do this right" or "this is how it works"
- Numbers anchor credibility: "3 minutes", "70% more moisture", "10 minutes, every morning"
- End with a question or CTA that earns a save or comment
- Hashtags: 5–8 max

**Niche:** #mensgrooming #skincareformen #bodycare #mensbodycare
**Broad:** #menswellness #groomingtips #morningroutine #gymlife

## Tone Reference

## Content Funnel — Broad to Specific

Start wide. Don't mention products or even rituals early. Build the problem first, earn trust, then introduce the system.

| Phase | Posts | Focus | Product mention? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | 1–6 | The gap. The problem. The stats. Men relate, share. | No |
| **Interest** | 7–12 | The system exists. There's a better way. Tease the ritual. | Vague reference |
| **Consideration** | 13+ | The SOLUM method. Specific products. How it works. | Yes |

---

## CTA Structure — Every Post

Every post drives to the website via link in bio. Structure:

- **Post body:** Educates or provokes. Ends on an open loop — "here's the problem / here's what changes."
- **Caption CTA:** "The full system is at the link in bio." or "Find out what you're missing → link in bio."
- **Never:** "Buy now." Not yet. Awareness phase = curiosity, not conversion.
