## 3. TWO-TIER RITUAL SYSTEM

### Daily Ritual — 10 Minutes (Every Morning)

1. **Product 04 — Scalp Massager** · Place flat on scalp, small firm circles from hairline to back. 2–3 min.
2. **Product 01 — Body Wash** · Apply to wet skin, chest down. No sulphates. Lifts dirt without removing natural oils.
3. **Product 02 — Exfoliating Mitt** · Wet the mitt, add body wash. Long circular strokes on arms, chest, stomach, legs. Medium pressure. Korean bathhouse technique.
4. **Product 03 — Back Scrub Cloth** · One handle each hand. Drape over shoulder. Saw back and forth — upper, mid, lower back. 60 seconds.
5. **Product 07 — Body Lotion** · Apply within 3 minutes of towelling. Skin absorbs 70% more moisture while still warm. Two pumps. Press in, don't rub.

### Weekly Deep Ritual — 22 Minutes (Replaces Daily)

1. **Product 04 — Scalp Massager** · Deep scalp massage, 5 minutes. More time, more pressure than daily.
2. **Product 05 — Rhassoul Clay Mask** · Apply to damp skin head to toe. Leave 8–10 minutes. Atlas Mountain clay draws out impurities.
3. **Product 08 + 03 — Kese Mitt + Back Cloth** · Firm slow strokes top to bottom simultaneously. Dead skin will roll off.
4. **Product 06 — Argan Oil** · Rinse, towel lightly — stay damp. 10–15 drops pressed into skin. Don't rub — press and hold. No lotion needed today.

### The Four Levers That Drive Everything

1. **Churn Rate (most important):** The difference between conservative and optimistic is almost entirely churn — 7.5% vs 5.5%. That 2pp difference produces a £18.5M swing in 5-year profit.
2. **Organic Mix:** Raising organic acquisition from 20% to 35% effectively doubles your paid budget for free. Every viral hygiene education post reduces effective CAC.
3. **Ad Spend:** Because CAC payback is Month 1, every pound of ad spend is recovered immediately. The model is self-funding from Year 1.
4. **Kit Mix:** Moving customers from Daily Starter (£42) to Full Ritual (£78) increases first-order GP by £30.80. A 10pp shift in kit mix from Starter → Ritual is worth ~£80 extra LTV per customer.
